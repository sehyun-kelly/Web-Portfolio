adoptoy is a web application developed by myself and my peer for BCIT Winter Hacks 2021. It is a platform like a marketplace to give out or exchange toys for kids. Health Guard is a web application developed by team of 3 students including myself for BCIT COMP1800 course. The purpose of this app is to allo users to actively keep track of their health. Three main functionalies includes the followings: 1) Check the suggested screenings based on user's gender and age, 2) Set up schedules for the screenings and add to calendars, 3) Upload and view health checkup test results.
